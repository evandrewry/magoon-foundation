Dr. Samar Musmar 2013

In October of 2013, the Magoon Foundation made it possible for Dr. Samar Musmar, the only US-trained and board-certified family physician in Palestine, to travel to Baltimore to serve as a plenary speaker at an annual global health conference organized by the American Academy of Family Physicians. Dr. Musmar is the Vice-Dean of the An Najah Medical School in Nablus and the director of the first Family Medicine residency program in Palestine, which she founded in 2010. I was fortunate to attend the conference and meet with Dr. Musmar during her visit. She illustrated the geopolitical tensions and logistical impediments that complicate the development of family medicine in Palestine. Not only does the region face the humanitarian needs of a developing country but also must deal with the non-communicable diseases common in the developed world.
